Veg Fried Rice

A quick and tasty rice dish made with vegetables and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  1. Cooked rice
  2. Carrot
  3. Beans
  4. Soy sauce
  5. Oil

Recipe

Heat oil in a pan and sauté vegetables. Add cooked rice and mix well. Add soy sauce and salt. Stir-fry on high flame for 3–4 minutes. Serve hot.
