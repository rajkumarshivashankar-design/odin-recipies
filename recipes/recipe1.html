Veg Pizza

A simple homemade veg pizza loaded with fresh vegetables and cheese.

Ingredients

  1. Wheat flour
  2. Capsicum
  3. Onion
  4. Cheese
  5. Pizza sauce

Recipe

Prepare the dough and let it rise. Roll it out evenly. Spread pizza sauce on the base. Add chopped vegetables and cheese. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C for 12–15 minutes. Slice and serve hot.
