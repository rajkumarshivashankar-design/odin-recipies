Paneer Butter Masala

A rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer and butter.

Ingredients

  1. Paneer
  2. Butter
  3. Tomato puree
  4. Fresh cream
  5. Spices

Recipe

Heat butter in a pan and add tomato puree. Cook with spices until oil separates. Add paneer cubes and fresh cream. Simmer for 5 minutes. Serve hot with roti or naan.
